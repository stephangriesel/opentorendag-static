Who will take the baton for the Open Tower Day Amsterdam?

A call to cultural organizations, foundations, architecture lovers, and event organizers in Amsterdam.

Since 2013, the Open Tower Day Amsterdam (OTD) has been a highlight on Amsterdam's cultural calendar. With thousands of enthusiastic visitors per edition, this unique event offers views across the city and a rare look behind the scenes of both historical and modern towers.

The current Stichting Open The Doors has made this event a great success with much passion. However, to ensure continuity, the current board is looking for a suitable party to take over the organization.

We are looking for a new organizer who:

  • Shares the vision: Love for architecture, history, and making Amsterdam's towers accessible to a wide audience.
  • Has experience: Affinity with event organization, volunteer management, and cultural projects.
  • Has the resources: The capacity and ambition to let the OTD grow further.
  • Uses the network: Can maintain relationships with participating towers, the City of Amsterdam, and sponsors.

What we offer:

A solid foundation with a proven organizational structure, a strong brand, an active network of partners, and a clear blueprint for future editions. The board is happy to facilitate a smooth transfer.

Interested in a conversation?

Please contact Henri van Poll, chairman of the Open The Doors foundation.
